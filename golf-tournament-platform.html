FAQ & Support
Find answers or chat with our support bot below
For Players
How do I find tournaments near me? +
Use the search on the home page or the Tournaments tab. Enter your city or ZIP code and choose a radius. You can filter by format, handicap requirement, date range, and entry fee.
How does handicap integration work? +
Enter your USGA Handicap Index when registering. The system validates it and uses it for automatic net scoring, flight assignments, and handicap adjustments per USGA guidelines. Organizers can set a maximum handicap per event.
Can I register my whole team at once? +
Yes! After you register, you'll get a unique team link to share with your playing partners. Each person registers individually, but you're linked as a group. The organizer can see your team assignments in their dashboard.
What notifications will I receive? +
Registration confirmation, payment receipt, tee time assignment, rules sheet, morning-of reminder with directions, live scoring updates (where available), and final results. Choose your preferred channel: email, SMS, or WhatsApp.
What if I need to cancel my registration? +
Each tournament has its own refund policy set by the organizer, which is displayed on the tournament page before you register. Most events allow full refunds up to 7 days before the event.
For Organizers
How do I receive payments? +
Connect your Stripe account in Settings. Registration fees are collected at time of payment and deposited directly to your connected bank account, typically within 2 business days. TeeTime charges a small platform fee per registration.
How does the private pre-launch link work? +
When you create your tournament, it starts in Private mode. You get a secret link to share with loyal golfers. They can register immediately. The tournament only appears in public search results after you switch it to Public, or on a scheduled date.
Can I upload my own branding? +
Absolutely. Upload your organization's logo and a custom cover image for your tournament page. These appear on the public listing, your tournament detail page, registration confirmations, and notifications sent to players.
How does the course communication tool work? +
Search our database of 2,800+ courses nationwide. Send a standardized availability request directly through the platform. Courses can respond with available dates and pricing. Confirmed details populate into your tournament page automatically.

💳 Stripe Payment Integration
Create a Stripe account and enable payouts
Sign up at stripe.com, complete identity verification, and connect a bank account for payouts. Enable Stripe Connect for multi-party payments so organizers receive funds directly.
Required
Implement Stripe Connect (Express or Standard)
Each tournament organizer needs their own Stripe connected account. Use Stripe Connect Express for the easiest onboarding. TeeTime takes a platform fee; organizers receive the rest automatically.
Required
Build the payment flow with Stripe Checkout or Payment Intents
Use Stripe Payment Intents API on your backend. Never handle raw card data — Stripe's hosted fields keep you PCI compliant. Add webhook listeners for payment_intent.succeeded, charge.refunded, etc.
Required
Set up webhook endpoint for real-time payment events
A backend endpoint (e.g. /api/webhooks/stripe) listens for Stripe events and triggers registration confirmations, dashboard updates, and email receipts.
Required
Implement refund logic and cancellation policies
Allow organizers to define refund windows. Build admin UI to issue full or partial refunds. Stripe's Refunds API handles the actual money movement.
Important
Add Stripe Tax for automatic tax calculation (optional)
If tournaments are taxable events in your jurisdiction, Stripe Tax can calculate and collect the right amount automatically.

🏌️ USGA Ratings, Slopes & Handicaps
License the GHIN API for handicap verification
GHIN (Golf Handicap and Information Network) is the USGA's official system. Their API lets you look up a player's verified Handicap Index by GHIN number. Requires a licensing agreement with the USGA.
Required
Source official Course Rating & Slope from USGA database
The USGA maintains the authoritative course rating and slope database. You can license this data or pull it via Golf Genius or a similar data partner. Replace all approximated ratings in the course DB.
Required
Build handicap calculation engine
Implement the USGA/R&A World Handicap System formula: take the best 8 of last 20 score differentials. Apply course handicap calculation (Handicap Index × Slope / 113 + Course Rating − Par).
Required
Implement scramble team handicap formulas
4-man scramble: 10% of A + 15% of B + 20% of C + 25% of D. 2-man scramble: 35% low + 15% high. These are standard but organizers may customize.
Important
Explore Golf Genius or GOLF.com API for scoring data
Golf Genius is the industry standard for tournament scoring. They offer API integrations. GolfNow/NBC Sports Golf has course availability and tee time data worth exploring.
Nice to Have
⛳ Course Database — Scale & Data Quality
License a national golf course dataset
Options: GolfNow API (~15,000 courses), Golf Channel/NBC Sports data feed, or iGolf / Golf Canada data. Expect licensing fees. Alternatively, scrape and manually verify a starter dataset of top 500 US tournament venues.
Required
Collect verified contact info for each course
Each course record needs: tournament/events coordinator email, phone number, booking URL, and whether they accept outside tournaments. This requires manual outreach or a data vendor. Start with NY State (700+ courses).
Required
Replace approximated ratings/slopes with verified USGA data
Every rating and slope in the current prototype is approximate. Each needs to be replaced with the official USGA-certified values for each set of tees (Blue, White, Red, etc.).
Required
Add course photos (real) and amenity data
Source actual course photos (license from the courses directly, or use Google Places Photos API). Verify amenity data: range, caddies, catering, cart availability, tournament packages.
Important
Build a "Claim Your Course" flow for course managers
Let course staff claim and manage their own listing — update contact info, photos, availability calendar, tournament pricing. Like a Google Business Profile for golf courses.
Nice to Have
⚙️ Backend, Auth & Infrastructure
Build authentication system (players + organizers)
Use Auth0, Clerk, or Firebase Auth for user management. Two user roles: Player and Organizer, with different dashboards and permissions. Add social login (Google, Apple) for easier registration.
Required
Choose and set up a backend framework + database
Recommended: Next.js (full-stack) + PostgreSQL (via Supabase or Railway) + Prisma ORM. Tables needed: users, tournaments, registrations, courses, payments, notifications, teams, reviews.
Required
Implement geolocation / radius search
Store lat/lng for each course and tournament. Use PostGIS (PostgreSQL extension) or a geospatial query to find tournaments within X miles of a user. Google Maps Geocoding API converts ZIP/city to coordinates.
Required
File storage for logos and cover images
Use AWS S3, Cloudflare R2, or Supabase Storage. Organizers upload images which are stored and served via CDN. Add image resizing/optimization (Cloudflare Images or imgix).
Required
📢 Notifications (Email / SMS / WhatsApp)
Set up transactional email via SendGrid or Postmark
For registration confirmations, receipts, tee time assignments, reminders, and results. Design branded email templates. Postmark is best for reliability; SendGrid for volume.
Required
Integrate Twilio for SMS notifications
Twilio's SMS API sends tee time reminders, morning-of alerts, and results. Requires a registered 10DLC number for US delivery. Budget ~$0.0079/SMS.
Important
Integrate WhatsApp Business API via Twilio or Meta
WhatsApp requires Meta Business verification and approved message templates. Twilio's WhatsApp sandbox is good for testing. Expect 1–4 week approval time for production access.
Nice to Have
Build notification preference center
Let users choose channel (email/SMS/WhatsApp) and types of notifications they want. Store preferences per user. Honor unsubscribes and comply with CAN-SPAM / TCPA regulations.
Important
⚖️ Legal & Compliance
Draft Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
Required before launch. Cover: user data handling, payment processing, organizer responsibilities, refund policies, liability limitations. Hire a lawyer or use a service like Termly as a starting point.
Required
Ensure PCI compliance via Stripe
By using Stripe's hosted fields and never touching raw card data, you maintain SAQ A compliance (the simplest PCI tier). Complete Stripe's annual compliance questionnaire.
